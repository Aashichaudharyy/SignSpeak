How It Works

SignSpeak translates sign language into text and speech in real-time, creating a seamless communication experience.

1

Gesture Detection

Flex sensors detect hand movements and finger positions with high precision, capturing the nuances of sign language gestures.

2

Translation Processing

The embedded Arduino microcontroller processes sensor data using our proprietary algorithm to identify and translate gestures into text.

3

Voice Output

Translated text is converted to natural-sounding speech through the integrated speaker, creating an instant voice for sign language users.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the translation?
SignSpeak achieves over 95% accuracy for standard ASL signs and commonly used phrases. The accuracy continues to improve as the glove learns your specific signing patterns.
Can I customize the voice output?
Yes, the companion app allows you to select from multiple voice options, adjust speaking speed, and personalize the accent to your preference.
How long does the battery last?
The SignSpeak glove provides approximately 12 hours of active use on a single charge. Standby time extends to several days with power-saving mode enabled.
Is the glove available in different sizes?
Yes, SignSpeak comes in small, medium, and large sizes to ensure comfort and accuracy. The glove includes adjustable straps for a snug fit.
Which sign languages are supported?
Currently, SignSpeak supports American Sign Language (ASL), British Sign Language (BSL), and Indian Sign Language (ISL). We're continuously expanding language support through software updates.
